Our Vision

Creating a regenerative destination for healing and connection

We create a regenerative destination to help people destress from daily life and reconnect with nature through short-term rentals, weekend retreats, and educational experiences.

Nestled at the base of the mountains, guests explore scenic trails by day, enjoy spa services and farm-to-table meals by night, and connect with like-minded people under the stars.
