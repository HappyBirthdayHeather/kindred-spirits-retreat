Reconnect with Nature

Permaculture & Regenerative Retreat at the Base of the Mountains

Book Your Stay

Vision

Create a regenerative destination to help people destress from daily life and reconnect with nature through short-term rentals, weekend retreats, and educational experiences.

Guests explore scenic trails by day, enjoy spa services and farm-to-table meals by night, and connect with like-minded people under the stars.

Values & Goals

Accountability

Take ownership of our impact on each other and the environment.

Community

Provide purpose and belonging to employees, guests, and neighbors.

Abundance

Share knowledge, resources, and space with surrounding communities.

Amenities

  • Lodging: Glamping tents, tipis, treehouses, cabins, yurts, domes, train cabins
  • RV Sites: Full hookups with electric and water
  • Communal Areas: Outdoor cooking, chuckwagon, community building
  • Wellness: Yoga, meditation, massages, sweat lodge
  • Recreation: Playground, tree nets, volleyball, hiking trails

Activities

  • ATV & horseback riding
  • Farm-to-table meals
  • Orchard and aquaponics tours
  • Yoga, breathwork, massages
  • Indigenous workshops & cultural events
  • Seasonal entertainment, campfires, stargazing

Development Plan

Year 1-3: Infrastructure, glamping, cabins, treehouses, RV sites, aquaponics, swimming pond, livestock.

Future Plans

  • Farm-to-table restaurant & event center
  • Additional greenhouses, yurts, domes, train cabins
  • Old West themed area
  • Guided excursions, long-term food security
