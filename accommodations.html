Stay in Harmony with Nature

Unique eco-friendly accommodations for every type of traveler

Glamping Tents

Luxury camping meets natural comfort

Canvas Wall Tents

Experience the perfect blend of outdoor adventure and indoor comfort in our spacious canvas wall tents, nestled in peaceful forest clearings.

  • Queen-size bed with luxury linens
  • Wood-burning stove for warmth
  • Solar-powered lighting
  • Private outdoor shower
  • Coffee station and mini-fridge
  • Private deck with seating

A-Frame Cabins

Cozy retreats with mountain views

Mountain View Cabins

Wake up to panoramic mountain vistas in our charming A-frame cabins, built with sustainable materials and designed for comfort.

  • Full kitchen and living area
  • Loft bedroom with mountain views
  • Full bathroom with hot shower
  • Fireplace for cozy evenings
  • Entertainment area (minimal tech)
  • Private forest surroundings

Treehouses

Elevated retreats in the forest canopy

Canopy Escapes

Sleep among the treetops in our thoughtfully designed treehouses, offering a unique perspective on the forest ecosystem.

  • 12-15 feet above ground
  • Comfortable sleeping for 2-4 guests
  • Wildlife viewing deck
  • Spiral staircase access
  • Minimal environmental impact
  • Unobstructed stargazing views
