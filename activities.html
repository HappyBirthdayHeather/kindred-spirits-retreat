Experiences That Transform

Reconnect with nature through meaningful activities and wellness programs

Wellness & Mindfulness

Heal your body, mind, and spirit in nature's embrace

Forest Yoga & Meditation

Start your day with sunrise yoga sessions in our dedicated forest clearings, followed by guided meditation to center your mind and spirit.

  • Daily sunrise and sunset sessions
  • Natural outdoor studio spaces
  • Expert certified instructors
  • All skill levels welcome

Farm-to-Table Dining

Taste the difference of truly fresh, locally grown ingredients

Organic Culinary Experiences

Enjoy meals crafted from ingredients harvested the same day, prepared by chefs who understand the connection between soil and plate.

  • 100% organic ingredients from our farm
  • Seasonal menus that change weekly
  • Wood-fired outdoor kitchen
  • Herbal teas and fermented beverages

Educational Farm Tours

Learn the principles of regenerative agriculture and permaculture

Permaculture & Sustainable Farming

Discover how we grow food sustainably, manage resources, and work with nature rather than against it through hands-on learning experiences.

  • Guided tours of production areas
  • Composting and waste management
  • Aquaponics system demonstrations
  • Hands-on workshops available

Nature Adventures

Explore the natural beauty of Arkansas through active recreation

Swimming & Water Activities

Cool off in our natural swimming pond, complete with waterfall features and crystal-clear water filtered by aquatic plants.

  • Natural chemical-free swimming
  • Waterfall and natural filtration
  • Scenic mountain views
  • Perfect for hot Arkansas summers
