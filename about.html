Our Story

From vision to reality - creating Arkansas's regenerative retreat

Our Mission

Creating a regenerative destination for healing and connection

We believe that healing happens when people reconnect with nature, community, and themselves. Our mission is to create a regenerative destination that helps people destress from daily life and discover the profound peace that comes from living in harmony with the natural world.

Nestled at the base of the Arkansas mountains, we offer more than just accommodation – we provide a transformative experience where guests can explore scenic trails by day, enjoy spa services and farm-to-table meals by night, and connect with like-minded people under star-filled skies.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Accountability

We take ownership of our impact on each other and the environment through permaculture and regenerative techniques. Every decision we make considers its effect on the land, our community, and future generations.

Community

We provide purpose and belonging to employees, guests, and neighbors while fostering connection and education. Our retreat serves as a hub for bringing together people who share a vision of sustainable living.

Abundance

We share knowledge, resources, and space to create a thriving network with surrounding communities. Through abundance thinking, we believe there's enough for everyone when we work together.

Our Journey

From dream to destination - the story of our development

The Vision (2020)

Inspired by the need for spaces that heal both people and planet, we began dreaming of a regenerative retreat that could demonstrate sustainable living while providing transformative experiences for guests.

Finding Home (2021)

After extensive searching, we discovered 50 acres of pristine land at the base of the Arkansas mountains – the perfect location for our regenerative retreat with natural springs, diverse ecosystems, and breathtaking views.

Breaking Ground (2022)

We began implementing permaculture design principles, establishing food forests, creating natural swimming areas, and building our first accommodations using sustainable materials and methods.

Opening Doors (2024)

Phase 1 of our retreat opened to welcome our first guests, featuring glamping sites, RV hookups, and the beginning of our farm-to-table dining program with vegetables grown on-site.

Growing Forward (2025)

We're expanding with A-frame cabins, treehouses, and enhanced wellness programs while continuing to refine our regenerative practices and deepen our impact on the community.

Our Commitment to Sustainability

How we practice what we preach

Renewable Energy

Solar panels and micro-hydro systems provide clean energy for all operations

Zero Waste Goal

Comprehensive composting, recycling, and waste reduction programs

Water Conservation

Rainwater harvesting, greywater systems, and natural wastewater treatment

Carbon Sequestration

Food forests and regenerative practices that capture carbon in soil

Organic Farming

Permaculture principles guide all food production without chemicals

Local Economy

Supporting neighboring farms, artisans, and sustainable businesses
